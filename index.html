About us & What we do

Agro-tide Limited is a company limited by shares that was incorporated in Uganda for the purpose of farming, capacity building of farmers, agricultural produce processing, marketing and general improvement of the lives of the people involved either indirectly or directly in the projects.

Business premises are Located in Soroti town in eastern Uganda on Moroto road with a branch in Arua District. Agro tide limited has been in operation since 2013.

Agricultural Produce

We buy produce mainly sorghum, maize, simsim, soya millet from rural based farmers at relatively higher prices than other produce dealers

Value Addition

We play a key role in value-added processing and marketing of agricultural commodities. We export our produce to Sudan, Kenya and Rwanda.

Farmer Trainings

We mobilize rural farmers to grow quality agricultural produce. The farmers are provided with quality seed, basic training in post harverst management .

Warehouse Storage

We provide storage and Warehousing facilities for our clients and farmers and even transport their produce..

We realized that climate change is negatively impacting on agricultural productivity; this has influenced us to embark on agro-forestry projects. We have embarked on planting of pine trees which is still on going.
