Get Involved, Get Innovated

Agro-tide Ltd is a company limited by shares that was incorporated in Uganda for the purpose of farming, capacity building of farmers, agricultural produce processing, marketing and general improvement of the lives of the people involved either indirectly or directly in the projects. Business premises are Located in Soroti town in eastern Uganda on Moroto road with a branch in Arua District. Agro tide limited has been in operation since 2013.

Agricultural Produce

We buy produce mainly sorghum, maize, simsim, soya millet from rural based farmers at relatively higher prices than other produce dealers

Value Addition

We play a key role in value-added processing and marketing of agricultural commodities. We export our produce to Sudan, Kenya and Rwanda.

Farmer Trainings Support

We mobilize rural farmers to grow quality agricultural produce. The farmers are provided with quality seed, basic training in post harverst management .

Warehousing facilities

We provide storage and Warehousing facilities for our clients and farmers and even transport their produce.

Why do We Exist

Agro-tide limited primarily deals in agricultural produce with a bias in grains and pulses. The company mobilizes rural farmers to grow quality agricultural produce (green grams, sesame, soya bean, sorghum, maize, millet e.t.c).

Our Vision

To partner with governmental, commercial, international private entities in realizing strategic food security and achieving long-term growth and sustainability.

Our Mission

To grow into a sustainable agricultural and food products provider and partner, ensuring supply sufficiency, high quality of farming and production operations.

Service Excellence

Building an organizational culture based on service and operational excellence in thought and in execution.

Social Responsibility

And saving the best for last, our principal core value is our belief in God and entrusting his guidance to build our company to the benefit of society
